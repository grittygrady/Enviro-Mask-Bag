WHY ENVIRO MASK BAG?

The Enviro Mask Bag is an ideal storage option and is multi universal to fit any face piece. The bag has two clips and an attached D-Ring to attach to a SCBA harness or truck belt. There are multiple ways to hold, clip and hang a facepiece for the best positioning and comfort. Additionally, the regulator can be attached inside the bag for faster emergency donning at a moments notice. There is a one inch velcro closure to assure a tight closure. This is the most versatile bag on the market today!
